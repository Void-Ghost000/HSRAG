# RQ7 Claim Boundary

RQ7 is a benchmark scaffold for retrieval boundary control.

Allowed:
- compare global search, CTHC-pruned search, and unique address lookup
- measure correctness, false allow, token cost, P99 latency, ESI, auditability
- report candidate reduction and route determinism

Not allowed:
- claim HSRAG replaces all RAG systems
- claim unique address lookup handles all natural language queries
- claim production readiness
- claim legal advice
